About Me

Research Interest

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Animal Sciences at the University of Florida. My primary research interest lies in developing and implementing advanced artificial intelligence models to enhance the efficiency and sustainability of livestock farming, such as using deep learning-based computer vision models for animal body weight estimation, disease detection, and pose estimation.
